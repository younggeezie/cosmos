Firewalking

Firewalking is the act of walking barefoot over a bed of hot embers or stones.

Sifu Lawrence hosts Fire-Walking Seminars during special events. One of his most popular events was held on March 24, 2007 to help fundraise for Stanley Park and the windstorm damage it had caused.
