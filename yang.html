Yang Style Tai Chi

Tai Chi is an internal Chinese martial art, practiced for both its defense training and its health benefits.

Yang style Tai Chi orginates from the Yang school of Tai Chi. Of the 5 original schools of Tai Chi, Yang style is the 2nd oldest.

It is the most popular and widely practiced style of Tai Chi in the world.
