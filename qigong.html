Qi-Gong

Qi-Gong, sometimes spelled Chi-Kung (and pronounced chee-gung) is an ancient Chinese method to cultivate internal vital life-force to assist in healing.

Various techniques, including:

  • Breathing techniques
  • Postures
  • Meditations
  • Guided imagery

Qi means "breath" or "air" and is considered the "vital-life-force" or "life-force energy." Qigong practitioners believe that this vital-life-force penetrates and permeates everything in the universe.

Gong means "work" or "effort" and is the commitment an individual puts into any practice or skill that requires time, patience, and repetition to perfect.

Through study, the individual aims to develop the ability to manipulate Qi in order to promote self-healing, prevent disease, and increase longevity.
