Sifu Lawrence Der

Sifu Lawrence Der is the President of Cosmos Chiology Society of Vancouver.

He has over fifty years of experience teaching Qi Gong and has taught Tai Chi at the Chinese Cultural Center for over four decades.

He is a student of the late Sifu Tchoung Ta Tchen since 1972 and continues to practice and teach daily.
